1. Context & Problem Description

At 14:05 UTC, Engine Room 2 reported a catastrophic failure of the cooling pump secondary valve. Under standard Operating Procedure (SOP-99), this requires an immediate vessel-wide power down for component replacement. However, current positioning is within the High Traffic Corridor (HTC-Beta) with a Category 4 storm front approaching within 45 minutes. A full power down would leave the vessel without propulsion or stabilized heading in extreme weather conditions.
